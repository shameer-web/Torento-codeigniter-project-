About Us

With the growing need of finding professional assistance that explores and supports the aspirants of Canadian Immigration, To Toronto Inc comes into picture with a pledge to streamline the investing and educational processes. We have analyzed a significant gap between finding investment opportunities and initiating the immigration process. Similarly, in terms of student immigration, the lack of proper career counselling and the professional support once the student has come to Canada has been a major drawback. One of the consequences of such shortcoming would be the possibilities of losing financial stability while the student is pursuing his/her studies. While providing the facilitation of immigration support through our trusted immigration consultants, we strive to go the extra mile and provide customized solutions for your business and career investments. A company is like a child to its owner and we play no less role than of a caretaker. Right from finding an appropriate investment to forecasting returns to starting the operational activities we stay with you throughout the process. Not to mention achieving your immigration goals along the way. With years of experience in managing major investments across Canada, we offer you the most personalized services and know exactly what it requires to get an efficient business set up in a foreign country. With 24x7 support we are always here for you. Career counselling is another one of our expertise. To simplify, consider the following questions: Which is the best stream that suits my long term objectives in Canada, Can someone get me a job when I reach Canada, I have completed my course, now how to I proceed further? If any of these questions intrigue you.
